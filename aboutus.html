About Us

"An organization making space travel accessable"

Space Tourism is a leading space travel company that provides unforgettable experiences for adventure seekers and space enthusiasts. Our mission is to make space travel accessible and affordable for everyone, and we strive to deliver the highest level of customer satisfaction.

Founded in 2015, we have taken thousands of travelers to space and have been recognized as one of the top space travel companies in the world. Our team consists of experienced astronauts, engineers, and space travel experts who are dedicated to providing a safe and exciting space experience.

Out of This World, OOTW is a company built for recreational space tourism. We came up with the idea when one of our founders wanted to open up space tourism to more people, and this is when we started. We first opened up to the public in 2015 and have been running for almost a decade! Our next goal is to make space travel more afforable for all!
